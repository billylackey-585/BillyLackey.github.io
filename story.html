The Journey: Bridging Strategy and Execution

My career has always been driven by a fundamental question: How can we use information to make better decisions? What began in technical engineering roles has evolved into a passion for Business Intelligence, where I focus on transforming raw data into actionable business strategy. I believe that data is only as valuable as the stories it tells and the clarity it provides to stakeholders.

Technical Mastery & Certification

I am a Microsoft Certified: Power BI Data Analyst Associate (PL-300), a credential that reflects my expertise in the entire data lifecycle—from ingestion and modeling to visualization and deployment. My technical toolkit includes:

  • Microsoft Ecosystem: Expert-level proficiency in Power BI, Power Query, and DAX for complex data modeling.
  • Data Engineering: Hands-on experience with SQL (MySQL) for data cleaning and Databricks/Python for scalable data processing.
  • Visualization: A dedicated focus on creating intuitive, high-impact dashboards in both Power BI and Tableau.

The "Growth Mindset" in Action

The data landscape never stops moving, and neither do I. Over the past year, I have completed a rigorous professional development path, mastering everything from Advanced DAX and Power BI Service Administration to Python for Data Science. This commitment to continuous learning ensures that the solutions I build are not just functional, but aligned with modern industry best practices.

Beyond the Data

When I’m not diving into a new dataset or refining a dashboard, I’m likely spending time with my family and our pets. I bring that same sense of dedication and loyalty to my professional work, striving to be a reliable partner who helps organizations see their data more clearly.
